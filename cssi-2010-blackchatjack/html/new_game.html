How to Play


Objective: Beat the dealer.


Cards are counted at face value, except for Aces (which are counted as 1 or 11).

Each player is dealt two cards, one face up, and one face down.

The goal of the game is to beat the dealer by getting a combined value 21.

You also win when you have a higher value than the dealer, or when the dealer busts.

After you get your cards, you can choose to HIT, or draw another card, or STAY, not drawing anymore cards.

The game ends when both players choose to stay.
